Tourist Places

Siddheshwar Temple

One of the most important spiritual landmarks in Solapur. Dedicated to Lord Siddheshwar, located near Siddheshwar Lake.

Pandharpur

Located 70 km away. Famous for Vitthal Rukmini Temple. Major pilgrimage during Ashadhi and Kartiki Ekadashi.

Akkalkot

Located 40 km away. Famous for Shri Swami Samarth Maharaj Temple.

Solapur Fort

Also called Bhuikot Fort. Built during Bahmani Sultanate period.
